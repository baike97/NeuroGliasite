Welcome Latter

Dear Friends and Colleagues,

We are pleased to announce the 3rd Edition of the Advanced School in Neurochemistry, to be held from November 20th to 24th, 2023, in Salvador, Bahia, in conjunction with the VIII International Symposium on Neurochemistry and Glial Cell Pathophysiology / VIII NeuroGlia.

The symposium is open to all interested students and professionals. In this edition, the Advanced School in Neurochemistry will be limited to an audience of 50 graduate students and will prioritize the participation of doctoral students and young scientists of black and indigenous backgrounds and female gender.

Three Technical Training courses will be offered, along with tutorial sessions, where young scientists can interact with a team of national and international collaborators, renowned researchers in their field.
